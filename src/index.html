Austin Back




Physics Masters Student at Clemson University

  I am a current master's student studying condensed matter and surface physics. I am currently finishing up my thesis research involving the study of ion beam irradiation on pure niobium to analyze the changes to its superconduction properties.

  I enjoy my current research because it allows me to combine many of the skills that I have learned and developed. I have been able to use my programming skills by working on extending the abilities of the SRIM simulation software by developing my own application to produce range plots of ion beams. I have also gotten the opportunity to apply my problem-solving and engineering skills to the development and installation of new lab equipment for my research. Finally, it has allowed me to refine my teamwork skills as the research has required me to work with multiple teams that I work with and keep updated on the project's progress.

  If you want to know more about me, send me a message or reach out to me at aback@clemson.edu.
